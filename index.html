Biography

I am currently a first-year PhD student supervised by Dr Rafal Mantiuk at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Cambridge. Prior to that, I was a researcher at Tencent working on computer vision problems. I received a MPhil in Computer Science and Engineering supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, and a BEng in Electronic Engineering with First Class Honours supervised by Prof. Kin-Man Lam at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
